Green Infrastructure Site Selection Criteria and Analysis | City of Urbana (In Progress)

The City of Urbana is facing flooding issues in different areas. Instead of combating the traditional way by building more drainage system, the city is seeking alternative method in green infrastructures. Many areas are available for building raining garden or bioswale in the city.

The goal is to identify the best locations for green infrastructure construction according to the different criteria set for this project. The following are the steps that I am going to take to accomplish the goal. The project duration will approximately be six months (Jan - Jul 2017)

  • Meet with stakeholders at the City of Urbana to discuss the implementation process and criteria

  • Collect all relevant GIS data and Excel data regarding tree inventory, flood complains, and utilities

  • Develop an overall site selection project flow chart and implementation guide line

  • Perform spatial analysis to determine the best locations

  • Develop a web applicaiton to visualize the result from the analysis

Parnter: City of Urbana - Type: GIS Analaysis and Webmap - Estimate Completion Date: July 2017
